public static X509Certificate createEphemeralCertificate(X500Principal subject,
                                                         KeyPair keyPair)
                                                  throws GeneralSecurityException
Creates a self-signed ephemeral certificate.

The resulting certificate will have a not-before date of yesterday, and not-after date of tomorrow.

Parameters:
subject - the subject to certify.
keyPair - the key pair to sign the certificate with.
Returns:
a new certificate.
Throws:
